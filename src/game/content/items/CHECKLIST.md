# Item Definition Checklist

- Use a unique `id` and clear player-facing `name`.
- Pick the correct `slot` and matching `gridW` / `gridH` footprint.
- Keep `basePrice` aligned with comparable items in `src/game/content/items/itemCatalog.js`.
- Use additive and multiplicative stats consistently with current item conventions.
- Keep the draft isolated until you are ready to wire it into live item data.
- Sanity-check sell value and affix interactions before promotion.
